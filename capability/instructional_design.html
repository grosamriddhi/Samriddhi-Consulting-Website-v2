Why Instructional Design

Samriddhi's instructional design practice applies systematic methodologies (rooted in instructional theories and models) to design and develop content, experiences, and evaluations to support the acquisition of new knowledge or skills – one that lasts beyond the learning environment and makes a sustainable impact on your business.
